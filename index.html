COVID19 VIETNAM/ COVID-19 tại Việt Nam

This page provides a series of dashboards providing the latest information on confirmed COVID-19 cases as well as the latest data on vaccination administration. All data has been provided by the Ministry of Heath. Since the 27th of April 2021, the number of Covid19 cases has seen risen within Bac Giang, Hochiminh City and in Vietnam as a whole. The occurrence of a case of infection in the community with a more transmissible variant of SARS-CoV-2 (Delta variant) and opportunities for wide community exposure, gave me a signal that this wave couldnt be easy to pass by. Since the middle of May with the new cases per day just below 200, it has been boosted up the transmission not only in southern provinces/states.

The date which information was last updated is noted with the relevant indicators and graphs. Disclaimer
